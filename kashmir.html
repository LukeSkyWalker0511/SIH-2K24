Kashmir

Welcome to Kashmir, a breathtaking destination renowned for its unparalleled natural beauty. Nestled in the northern reaches of the Indian subcontinent, this enchanting region boasts verdant valleys, majestic snow-capped mountains, and crystal-clear lakes. Known as "Paradise on Earth," Kashmir offers a serene escape with its charming houseboats, lush gardens, and vibrant local culture. Explore the picturesque landscapes of Srinagar, the adventure-filled terrains of Gulmarg, and the tranquil beauty of Pahalgam. Whether you seek peaceful retreats or thrilling outdoor activities, Kashmir promises an unforgettable experience amidst its stunning scenery and rich heritage.

MUST-visit Destinations:


Dal Lake:

Dal is a freshwater lake in Srinagar, the summer capital of the Indian-administered Jammu and Kashmir in the disputed Kashmir region. It is an urban lake, the second largest lake in Jammu and Kashmir, and the most visited place in Srinagar by tourists and locals

Avatipura Ruins

These ancient ruins are remnants of a Hindu temple complex built by King Avantivarman and offer insight into the region's historical and cultural heritage.

Betaab Valley:

Named after the Bollywood movie “Betaab,” this valley is famous for its lush greenery and stunning river views, making it a perfect spot for nature enthusiasts.

Gulmarg:

Renowned for its lush green meadows and as a premier skiing destination, Gulmarg also features the Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world.


Kashmir Tour Packages

Low-Budget Package

Duration: 5-6 days

Destinations:

  • Srinagar: Explore Dal Lake, Mughal Gardens (Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh), and the old city.
  • Gulmarg: Enjoy the scenic beauty and explore the meadows.
  • Pahalgam: Visit Betaab Valley and Aru Valley.

Accommodation:

  • Srinagar: Budget hotels or guesthouses.
  • Gulmarg: Budget hotels or basic lodges.
  • Pahalgam: Budget hotels or local guesthouses.

Estimated Cost:

$400 - $600 per person

Sample Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar, explore local attractions, stay in a budget hotel.
  • Day 2: Full day in Srinagar, visit Mughal Gardens, Dal Lake boat ride.
  • Day 3: Travel to Gulmarg, explore meadows, stay in a budget hotel.
  • Day 4: Travel to Pahalgam, visit Betaab Valley, overnight stay in Pahalgam.
  • Day 5: Explore Aru Valley or enjoy local activities, return to Srinagar.
  • Day 6: Departure from Srinagar.

  • The most efficient way to reach Kashmir is by flying into Srinagar Airport (Sheikh ul-Alam International Airport). It’s well-connected with major Indian cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore, offering a swift and comfortable start to your Kashmir adventure.

  • By Train 🚆

  • For a scenic journey, take a train to Jammu Tawi Railway Station. From Jammu, you can travel to Srinagar by road, enjoying the changing landscapes as you drive through lush valleys and mountainous terrain. Trains offer a relaxing way to soak in the Indian countryside before reaching Kashmir.
  • By Road 🛣

    • Driving to Kashmir is an adventurous option that allows you to experience stunning views of the Himalayas and picturesque towns along the way. Routes from cities like Delhi and Chandigarh provide a captivating road trip experience. Alternatively, various bus services operate between major cities and Kashmir, offering both comfort and convenience.
