SIKKIM

Sikkim, nestled in the Himalayas, is known for its diverse landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and serene monasteries. Home to Mount Kanchenjunga, the world’s third-highest peak, it offers breathtaking views and trekking opportunities. Sikkim's vibrant festivals, warm hospitality, and organic lifestyle make it a unique destination. Key attractions include Tsomgo Lake, Rumtek Monastery, and Yumthang Valley, showcasing the state's natural beauty and cultural richness.

MUST-visit Destinations:


Tsomgo Lake:

A glacial lake located at an elevation of 3,753 meters, known for its serene beauty and surrounded by snow-covered mountains.

Rumtek Monastery

The largest monastery in Sikkim, it's a spiritual and architectural marvel set against a backdrop of lush green mountains.

Yumthang Valley

Known as the 'Valley of Flowers', it is a paradise for nature lovers with its rich flora and stunning mountain views.

Kanchenjunga National Park:

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, it offers diverse wildlife and breathtaking landscapes, including the majestic Mount Kanchenjunga.


SIKKIM TOUR PLAN

Low-Budget Package

Duration: 4-5 days

DAY PLAN:

  • DAY 1: Visit Gangtok, the capital of Sikkim. Explore the local markets and enjoy the evening at MG Marg.
  • DAY 2: Head to Tsomgo Lake and Baba Mandir. Enjoy the scenic beauty and traditional yak rides.
  • DAY 3: Travel to Lachung. Visit the Yumthang Valley, also known as the Valley of Flowers.
  • DAY 4: Visit the Gurudongmar Lake, one of the highest lakes in the world, and return to Gangtok.

Accommodation:

    Budget Hotels & Guesthouses
  • Hotel Dew Pond - Affordable rooms with basic amenities.
  • New Ashok Residency - Budget-friendly accommodation near MG Marg.
  • The Orchid Glade - Cozy guesthouse with local hospitality.

Estimated Cost:

₹15,000 - ₹25,000 per person

Mid-Budget Package

Duration: 6-7 days

Accommodation:

  • The Royal Plaza - Centrally located with modern amenities and great views.
  • Summit Norling Resort & Spa - Comfortable resort with spa facilities, offering a luxurious stay.
  • Norkhill Hotel - A heritage hotel known for its royal ambiance and excellent service.

Estimated Cost:

₹35,000 - ₹50,000 per-person

Sample Itinerary:

  • Day 1-2: Follow the 4-day itinerary.
  • Day 3: Visit Pelling and explore attractions like the Pemayangtse Monastery and Rabdentse Ruins.
  • Day 4: Enjoy a day trip to Namchi, famous for its giant statue of Guru Padmasambhava and tea gardens.

High-Budget Package

Duration: 8-9 days

Accommodation:

  • Mayfair Spa Resort & Casino - Luxury spa resort with world-class facilities and a casino.
  • Elgin Nor-Khill - A historic hotel offering a blend of heritage and modern luxury.
  • WelcomHeritage Denzong Regency - Premium hill resort providing excellent views and service.

Estimated Cost:

₹50,000 and up

Sample Itinerary:

  • Days 1-2: Follow the mid-budget itinerary.
  • Day 3: Visit Yuksom, the historical capital of Sikkim, and the Dubdi Monastery.
  • Day 4: Explore the Khangchendzonga National Park and trek to the famous Goecha La Pass.
