MANALI

Manali, a popular hill station nestled in the northern state of Himachal Pradesh, is known for its breathtaking landscapes, snow-capped mountains, and lush greenery. It offers a blend of tranquility, adventure, and cultural experiences, with attractions like the Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, and Hadimba Temple. Manali is a go-to destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and those seeking a peaceful retreat.

MUST-visit Destinations:


Solang Valley:

A picturesque valley known for adventure sports such as paragliding and skiing.

Rohtang Pass:

A high mountain pass offering stunning views of the surrounding Himalayas.

Hadimba Temple:

An ancient cave temple surrounded by cedar forests, dedicated to the goddess Hadimba.

Vashisht Hot Springs:

Natural hot springs with therapeutic properties, located in the Vashisht village.


MANALI TOUR PLAN

Low-Budget Package

Duration: 3-4 days

DAY PLAN:

  • DAY 1: Arrival in Manali, local sightseeing including the Hadimba Temple and Old Manali.
  • DAY 2: Excursion to Solang Valley for adventure sports.
  • DAY 3: Visit to Vashisht Hot Springs and Mall Road for shopping and dining.

Accommodation:

    Budget Hotels & Hostels
  • Hotel Manali Castle - A cozy, budget-friendly hotel near the Mall Road.
  • The Lost Tribe Hostel - A popular hostel among backpackers with a vibrant atmosphere.
  • Hotel Beas View - Offers beautiful views of the Beas River at a reasonable price.

Estimated Cost:

₹10,000 - ₹15,000 per person

Mid-Budget Package

Duration: 5-6 days

Accommodation:

  • Johnson Lodge & Spa - A comfortable stay with spa facilities near Mall Road.
  • Snow Valley Resorts - Offers excellent views and amenities.
  • The Orchard Greens - A serene resort with beautiful orchards.

Estimated Cost:

₹20,000 - ₹30,000 per person

Sample Itinerary:

  • Day 1-2: Follow the 3-day itinerary.
  • Day 3: Trip to Rohtang Pass and Nehru Kund.
  • Day 4: Explore the Naggar Castle and the Nicholas Roerich Art Gallery.

High-Budget Package

Duration: 7-8 days

Accommodation:

  • The Himalayan - A luxury resort designed like a medieval castle with premium services.
  • Span Resort and Spa - Nestled by the river with extensive facilities.
  • ManuAllaya Resort - Offers high-end amenities with beautiful views of the Himalayas.

Estimated Cost:

₹40,000 and up

Sample Itinerary:

  • Days 1-2: Follow the mid-budget itinerary.
  • Day 3: Indulge in a spa day or wellness retreat at your luxury resort.
  • Day 4: Private tour of the Hampta Pass or the Great Himalayan National Park.
