DUBAI

Dubai is a vibrant city in the United Arab Emirates (UAE), known for its modern architecture, luxury shopping, and dynamic nightlife. It’s located on the southeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, along the Persian Gulf. The city is famous for landmarks like the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, and the Palm Jumeirah, an artificial archipelago. Dubai is also a major global business hub and a popular tourist destination, offering everything from traditional souks to futuristic skyscrapers. Its economy has diversified beyond oil, with significant sectors in tourism, real estate, and finance.

MUST-visit Destinations:


BURJ KHALIFA:

The tallest building in the world, offering breathtaking views from its observation decks on the 124th, 125th, and 148th floors.

DUBAI MALL

One of the world's largest shopping malls, featuring a vast array of shops, restaurants, an ice rink, and the Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo.

BURJ AL ARAB

Known as the "world's only seven-star hotel," its sail-shaped silhouette is a symbol of luxury and extravagance

DUBAI CREEK:

A natural saltwater creek that divides the city into Deira and Bur Dubai, offering traditional abra boat rides and historical sites like the Dubai Museum and the Al Fahidi Historic District.


DUBAI TOUR PLAN

Low-Budget Package

Duration: 3-4 days

DAY PLAN:

  • DAY 1: Explore major landmarks like the Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, and Dubai Fountain. Visit the Dubai Aquarium and take a stroll through Downtown Dubai.
  • DAY 2: Visit the historic Dubai Creek, take an abra ride, explore the Dubai Museum, and wander through the Al Fahidi Historic District. In the afternoon, head to Jumeirah Beach and visit the iconic Burj Al Arab.
  • DAY 3: Spend time in Dubai Marina, enjoy the waterfront, and visit Palm Jumeirah. You can also explore the Atlantis The Palm or have a day at leisure for shopping or relaxing..
