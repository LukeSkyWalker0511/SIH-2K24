SINGAPORE

Singapore, a vibrant city-state in Southeast Asia, is known for its multicultural population, stunning skyline, and efficient urban planning. It's a hub of modern architecture, green spaces, and cultural landmarks. Visitors can explore famous sites like Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, and Sentosa Island, offering a blend of luxury, entertainment, and natural beauty. Singapore's culinary scene is equally renowned, with a mix of Chinese, Malay, Indian, and international cuisines.

MUST-visit Destinations:


Gardens by the Bay:

A nature park spanning 101 hectares, known for its futuristic Supertree structures and the iconic Flower Dome and Cloud Forest conservatories.

Marina Bay Sands

A luxury resort with a hotel, casino, and the world's largest rooftop infinity pool. Offers spectacular views of the city.

Sentosa Island

A resort island with beaches, theme parks, and attractions like Universal Studios, the S.E.A. Aquarium, and the Merlion statue.

Chinatown:

A vibrant cultural district with temples, traditional shops, and eateries, offering a glimpse into Singapore's rich heritage.


SINGAPORE TOUR PLAN

Low-Budget Package

Duration: 3-4 days

DAY PLAN:

  • DAY 1: Visit Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, and the ArtScience Museum. End the day with a stroll along the Singapore River.
  • DAY 2: Explore Chinatown, Little India, and Kampong Glam. Enjoy street food at a hawker center.
  • DAY 3: Spend the day at Sentosa Island, visiting the beaches and attractions. End the trip with a visit to the Singapore Zoo or Night Safari.

Accommodation:

    Budget Hotels & Hostels
  • Hotel 81 - Affordable and conveniently located.
  • 5footway.inn Project Chinatown: Offers dormitory-style accommodations in Chinatown.
  • Beary Best! Chinatown: A budget-friendly hostel in the heart of Chinatown.

Estimated Cost:

$400 - $700 per person

Mid-Budget Package

Duration: 6-7 days

Accommodation:

  • YOTEL Singapore Orchard Road - Located on Orchard Road, offers modern rooms and a rooftop pool.
  • Hotel G Singapore - Chic and centrally located with easy access to attractions.
  • Village Hotel Bugis - A well-placed hotel near Bugis Street, offering good value for its location.

Estimated Cost:

$900 - $1500 per-person

Sample Itinerary:

  • Day 1-2: Follow the 3-day itinerary.
  • Day 3: Visit the National Museum of Singapore and the Singapore Botanic Gardens.
  • Day 4: Explore modern attractions like the Singapore Flyer and Esplanade - Theatres on the Bay.

High-Budget Package

Duration: 7-8 days

Accommodation:

  • Raffles Singapore - A historic hotel known for its luxury and iconic architecture.
  • Capella Singapore - A luxury resort on Sentosa Island with private villas and exquisite amenities.

Estimated Cost:

$2500 and up

Sample Itinerary:

  • Days 1-2: Follow the mid-budget itinerary.
  • Day 3: Enjoy a high tea experience at a luxury hotel, and indulge in a day spa at one of Singapore’s top establishments.
