MUST-visit Destinations:


Elephant Beach (Havelock Island):

This beach is famous for its water sports and snorkeling opportunities. The clear blue waters and vibrant coral reefs make it a popular spot for underwater photography.

Radhanagar Beach (Havelock Island):

Known for its pristine white sand and crystal-clear waters, Radhanagar Beach is often cited as one of the most beautiful beaches in Asia. The sunsets here are particularly breathtaking.

North Bay Island

Known for its coral reefs and vibrant marine life, North Bay Island is a great spot for underwater photography and capturing the beauty of the marine ecosystem.

Cellular Jail (Port Blair):

This historical site is an important part of India's colonial history. Photos of the imposing structure and its exhibits can evoke a sense of the past.

    • Major Airports:

    • Veer Savarkar International Airport (Port Blair): The primary airport serving the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, located in the capital city of Port Blair. Most travelers arrive here.

    Airlines:

    • Domestic Airlines: Several Indian airlines operate flights to Port Blair from major cities such as Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, and Bengaluru. Airlines include Air India, GoAir, IndiGo, and SpiceJet.

    Booking Tips:

    • Advance Booking: Flights to the islands can be limited, so booking in advance is advisable.
    • Direct Flights: Look for direct flights to avoid layovers, as indirect routes may add significant travel time.
  • By Sea

    • Passenger Ships:

    • From Chennai: Regular passenger ship services operate from Chennai to Port Blair. The journey takes around 3-4 days.
    • From Kolkata: There are also passenger ship services from Kolkata to Port Blair, taking about 3-4 days.
    • From Visakhapatnam: Ships from Visakhapatnam to Port Blair are available, with a journey time of about 2-3 days.

    Booking Tips:

    • Advance Reservations: Ship tickets should be booked well in advance, especially during peak travel seasons.
    • Indian Maritime Schedule: Check schedules and availability on the official website of the Directorate of Shipping Services (DSS) or through authorized travel agents.
  • By Private Yacht or Boat

    • For Private or Luxury Travel:

    • Private Yachts: Luxury travelers or those seeking a more exclusive experience may opt for private yachts or boats. This is typically more expensive and requires special arrangements.

    Booking Tips:

    • Regulations: Ensure compliance with maritime regulations and obtain necessary permits.
    • Charter Services: Engage with yacht charter companies that operate in the region for detailed arrangements.
