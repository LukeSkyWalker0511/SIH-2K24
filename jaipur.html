Jaipur

Jaipur, the vibrant capital of Rajasthan, is renowned for its majestic forts, palaces, and colorful bazaars. Known as the "Pink City" for its distinct pink-hued architecture, Jaipur boasts iconic landmarks such as the Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, and City Palace. Explore the bustling Johari and Bapu bazaars for exquisite handicrafts and traditional jewelry. Don't miss the astronomical wonders at Jantar Mantar and the serene beauty of the Jal Mahal, set against the backdrop of Man Sagar Lake. Jaipur's rich cultural heritage is reflected in its cuisine, vibrant festivals, and warm hospitality
