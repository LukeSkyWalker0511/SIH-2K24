RISHIKESH

Rishikesh, located in the Himalayan foothills, is a hub for spirituality and adventure. Known as the 'Yoga Capital of the World', it attracts yogis and spiritual seekers from around the globe. Rishikesh offers numerous ashrams, serene ghats, and thrilling river rafting experiences on the Ganges. Key attractions include the iconic Laxman Jhula, Triveni Ghat, and the Beatles Ashram, reflecting the town's unique blend of spirituality and natural beauty.

MUST-visit Destinations:


Laxman Jhula:

An iconic suspension bridge offering stunning views of the Ganges and the surrounding hills.

Triveni Ghat

A sacred bathing spot where the Ganga Aarti, an evening prayer ritual, is performed daily.

Beatles Ashram

The ashram where the Beatles stayed and composed music, now an attraction with its graffiti-covered walls.

River Rafting:

A thrilling adventure activity on the Ganges, offering varying levels of difficulty.


RISHIKESH TOUR PLAN

Low-Budget Package

Duration: 3-4 days

DAY PLAN:

  • DAY 1: Arrive in Rishikesh, visit Laxman Jhula and Parmarth Niketan Ashram.
  • DAY 2: Enjoy a morning yoga session, then head to Triveni Ghat for the evening Aarti.
  • DAY 3: Explore the Beatles Ashram and participate in adventure activities like river rafting.

Accommodation:

    Budget Hotels & Hostels
  • Zostel Rishikesh - A popular hostel with a friendly atmosphere.
  • Live Free Hostel - Affordable and vibrant, ideal for backpackers.
  • Hotel Yog Vashishth - A budget-friendly option with good amenities.

Estimated Cost:

₹10,000 - ₹20,000 per person

Mid-Budget Package

Duration: 5-6 days

Accommodation:

  • EllBee Ganga View - Offers beautiful views of the Ganges and modern amenities.
  • The Grand Shiva Resort & Spa - A comfortable resort with spa facilities.
  • Aloha on the Ganges - A riverside resort with excellent services and views.

Estimated Cost:

₹25,000 - ₹40,000 per person

Sample Itinerary:

  • Day 1-2: Follow the 3-day itinerary.
  • Day 3: Visit the Neer Garh Waterfall and enjoy an evening at the Ganga beach.
  • Day 4: Experience bungee jumping or zip-lining for an adrenaline rush.

High-Budget Package

Duration: 7-8 days

Accommodation:

  • Ananda in the Himalayas - A luxury spa resort offering wellness programs and breathtaking views.
  • The Roseate Ganges - A boutique resort with luxurious rooms and private decks.
  • Taj Rishikesh Resort & Spa - Premium accommodation with world-class facilities.

Estimated Cost:

₹50,000 and up

Sample Itinerary:

  • Days 1-2: Follow the mid-budget itinerary.
  • Day 3: Participate in an immersive yoga and meditation retreat.
  • Day 4: Enjoy a private guided tour of the Rajaji National Park.
